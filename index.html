AIGC

AIGC, or AI-Generated Content, refers to the use of artificial intelligence to generate content, which is considered to be a new content creation method after professionally generated content (PGC) and user generated content (UGC).

AI writing

AI writing technology can quickly generate high-quality text through simulation and analysis based on artificial intelligence, thereby significantly reducing the cost and time of copywriting creation.

AI painting

AI painting, as the name suggests, is to use artificial intelligence to paint, and it is one of the typical application scenarios for artificial intelligence-generated content. Its main principle is to collect a large number of existing works, analyze their content and style characteristics through algorithms, and finally generate new works, so algorithms are the core of AI painting.
